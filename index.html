Street Manifest 3.0

StreetMug is a blend of street, cosmos and technology.
A space for those who live on street – but want to hold something more than just a cup.

“The street looks down. We teach it to look forward.”

What is StreetMug?

An ecosystem for street people.
A platform that transforms classic cup collecting into a digital 3.0 space: profile, crypto tools and a place for creativity.

Why does it exist?

Because many people on street have talent, stories and huge potential —
they just lack the tools.
